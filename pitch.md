# ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot Pitch

## Problem Statement
The old student portal is hard to use because students need to click many menus to find information.

## Industry 5.0 Justification
The AI copilot helps students by giving fast answers through chat or voice.

## User Stories
1. As a student, I want to check my class schedule, so I know my classes.
2. As a student, I want to check my clearance, so I know my pending requirements.
3. As a student, I want to ask for announcements, so I stay updated.
